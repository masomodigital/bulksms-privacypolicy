Privacy Policy

Masomo Digital Library

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

1. Introduction

Masomo Digital Library is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details: Name, National ID number, date of birth, MPESA number.
  • Account information: Username, referral code, earnings.
  • Transaction records: SMS credit purchases, withdrawal history.
  • Device & access data: IP address, browser type, log times.
  • Communication content (when contacting support or via platform forms).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To register and manage your account.
  • To track referrals and promo code usage.
  • To process payments and withdrawals.
  • To ensure platform security and enforce rules.
  • To provide customer support and improve services.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Paystack)
  • MPESA for withdrawals
  • UjumbeSMS for SMS notifications
  • Authorities if legally required

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Google infrastructure (Apps Script & Sheets). We apply access controls, secure connections (HTTPS), and password protection to safeguard your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion (if not legally restricted)
  • Withdraw consent

To make a request, contact us via the email below.

7. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience and monitor performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.

8. Age Restriction

This platform is only for users aged 18 and above who hold a valid Kenyan National ID.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted here with the updated effective date.
